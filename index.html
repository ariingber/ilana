Materials:

Students are encouraged to purchase their own art supplies so that they can practice at home in between lessons. Some watercolors, drawing materials, and paper will happily be provided. If you would like to work with a specific art material (i.e. modeling clay, acrylic paint, jewelry making, recycled materials etc.), you may purchase the supplies and a curriculum will be tailored specifically to your child’s interests.
