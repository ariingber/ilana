At The Happy HeART studio we are not simply concerned with making beautiful art, we are also concerned with developing a beautiful heart.

Through mindfulness, yoga, and art making activities, it is our objective to foster student creativity, build self-esteem and resilience, teach children how to self-love and self care, and positively impact others to create a peaceful, compassionate, and loving world.

Our Teacher

Ilana received her masters in Art and Art Education from Teachers College, Columbia University. She specializes in social emotional learning. Ilana has taught in both the public school, private school, and museum settings.

She began her career at the Guggenheim Museum’s Learning through Art Program and Creativity Institute. Later she taught at Success Academy Charter School for 5 years and became the Art Content Lead, providing mentorship and professional development for 30 art teachers across the network of schools. In 2016 she traveled to Fiji to receive her 200-hour teacher training with YogaWorks, supplementing her Little Flower Yoga and Mindfulness certification for children. Ilana is an active member of the National Art Education Association and has presented at numerous national conventions on topics such as: Teaching Collaboration Skills through Painting Curriculum, Assessing Creativity, and Infusing Mindfulness Practices into the Art Studio.

Materials:

Students are encouraged to purchase their own art supplies so that they can practice at home in between lessons. Some watercolors, drawing materials, and paper will happily be provided. If you would like to work with a specific art material (i.e. modeling clay, acrylic paint, jewelry making, recycled materials etc.), you may purchase the supplies and a curriculum will be tailored specifically to your child’s interests.
