What We Practice

Practice Self Love and Compassion

“The worst enemy to creativity is self doubt”
– Slyvia Plath

• The Happy HeART studio infuses lessons on positive self-talk and mindfulness to awaken students’ access to their creativity.
• Every art class opens and closes with guided meditations to help students quiet self-limiting beliefs.
• At the Happy HeART studio we actively practice self-love, self-care, and positive self-talk to cheer ourselves on. We are reminded to replace our inner critic with our inner coach.
